About Mive

MIVE is a B2B marketplace that aims to reach out to all the unorganized fruits and vegetables foodchains across the nation and make the tedious task of stock procurement hassle free through the extensive use of technology. Anyone can procure stock with least possible efforts by using MIVE Android Application or by simply using MIVE WebApp

No more travelling and facing the hassles of wholesale markets or mandis

MIVE assures Best & Fresh Stock at best prices available in mandi & timely delivered as per the requirements of buyers. The advanced logistics enables timely delivery at doorstep with ulmost care of stock. Thus with MIVE one not only save time and money but also reduces the human effort which could be utilized for some other productive work.
